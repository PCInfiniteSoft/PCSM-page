Features
Everything Automated

A complete system built around one goal — consistent, high-quality content with minimal human effort.

🤖

AI Content Planning

Gemini AI analyses brand voice, upcoming festivals, and market trends to generate weekly content plans with captions and creative angles for every SKU.

🎬

Automated Video Production

Lifestyle and product-review videos generated via Seedance and Creatify APIs — storyboard, render, and upload to Google Drive without touching a timeline.

📅

Smart Scheduling

APScheduler triggers posts at peak-engagement windows per platform. Each brand has its own posting calendar, timezone-aware and fully configurable.

🎵

BGM Matching

Mood-to-music mapping selects copyright-safe background tracks based on content theme — family gathering, street food, festival, everyday & more.

📊

Analytics & Feedback Loop

Engagement data (likes, shares, views, comments) is pulled back after every post and stored in SQLite. High-performing angles inform the next week’s plan.

🖥

Web Dashboard

FastAPI dashboard at port 8000 for monitoring the pipeline, managing API credentials, reviewing the post queue, and tailing live logs — no SSH needed.
